Chest Workout - Step by Step

Exercise 1: Bench Press

+

Sets: 4

Reps: 8-10

Rest: 90-120 seconds

Instructions: Lie flat on bench, grip the bar with hands shoulder-width apart. Lower the bar to your chest and press it back up explosively.

Exercise 2: Incline Dumbbell Press

+

Sets: 3

Reps: 10-12

Rest: 60-90 seconds

Instructions: Sit on an incline bench set at 45 degrees. Press dumbbells upward and slightly forward with controlled movement.

Exercise 3: Cable Flyes

+

Sets: 3

Reps: 12-15

Rest: 45-60 seconds

Instructions: Stand on a machine, keep a slight bend in your elbows, and bring your hands together in a controlled arc motion.

Back Workout - Step by Step

Exercise 1: Deadlift

+

Sets: 4

Reps: 6-8

Rest: 120-150 seconds

Instructions: Stand with feet hip-width apart, grip the bar at shoulder-width. Lift with your legs, keeping your back straight.

Exercise 2: Bent-Over Rows

+

Sets: 3

Reps: 10-12

Rest: 90-120 seconds

Instructions: Bend forward at hips with a slight knee bend. Pull the bar up to your lower chest with elbows close to your body.

Exercise 3: Pull-ups

+

Sets: 3

Reps: 8-12

Rest: 90-120 seconds

Instructions: Grip the bar with hands slightly wider than shoulder-width. Pull yourself up until chin is over the bar, then lower with control.

Legs Workout - Step by Step

Exercise 1: Squats

+

Sets: 4

Reps: 8-10

Rest: 120-150 seconds

Instructions: Place bar on shoulders, feet shoulder-width apart. Lower your body as if sitting in a chair, then push back up.

Exercise 2: Leg Press

+

Sets: 3

Reps: 10-12

Rest: 90-120 seconds

Instructions: Sit on the machine with feet on the platform at shoulder-width. Push the platform away from you, then return with control.

Exercise 3: Leg Curls

+

Sets: 3

Reps: 12-15

Rest: 60-90 seconds

Instructions: Lie face down on the machine. Curl your legs up towards your glutes, squeezing at the top before lowering.

Shoulders Workout - Step by Step

Exercise 1: Military Press

+

Sets: 4

Reps: 8-10

Rest: 90-120 seconds

Instructions: Stand with the bar at shoulder height. Press the bar overhead until arms are fully extended, then lower back down.

Exercise 2: Lateral Raises

+

Sets: 3

Reps: 12-15

Rest: 60-90 seconds

Instructions: Hold dumbbells at your sides with arms slightly bent. Raise them out to the sides until parallel with the floor.

Exercise 3: Reverse Peck Deck

+

Sets: 3

Reps: 12-15

Rest: 60-90 seconds

Instructions: Sit on the machine facing inward. Pull the handles toward you in a controlled arc, squeezing your rear shoulders.
